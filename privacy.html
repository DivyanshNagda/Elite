Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Introduction

Welcome to Elite Android Game Booster ("Elite", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our Android application.

Information We Collect

Elite is designed with privacy in mind and collects minimal information:

  • Device Information: Basic device specifications (resolution, DPI settings) to optimize game performance
  • App Usage Data: Settings and preferences you configure within the app
  • Performance Metrics: Anonymous performance data to improve app functionality

We do NOT collect: Personal information, browsing history, game content, or any data that could identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Providing and improving the Elite app functionality
  • Optimizing game performance settings for your device
  • Fixing bugs and enhancing user experience
  • Ensuring app stability and reliability

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly choose to share crash reports or feedback. We implement appropriate security measures to protect any data we may receive.

Third-Party Services

Elite integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Shizuku: For system-level permissions (no data shared)
  • GitHub: For app distribution and updates (handled by GitHub's privacy policy)

We do not share your personal information with any third-party services.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we may have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any data collection (though this may limit app functionality)
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
Important Note: Elite requires Shizuku permissions to function properly. These permissions are used solely for device optimization and are not used to collect or transmit personal data. All operations are performed locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Elite is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • GitHub: github.com/DivyanshNagda/EliteGaming
  • Issues: GitHub Issues

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date listed above and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future.
